Medical Disclaimer

Important information about the nature of content on this website.

🚨 Emergency Notice

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger or experiencing a psychiatric emergency, please call 08005 49782 or 6350180497 immediately, or go to the nearest emergency room. Do not rely on website content in emergencies.

Not a Substitute for Professional Care

The content on this website — including articles, descriptions of conditions, treatment information, and self-assessment tools — is provided for general information and educational purposes only. It is NOT intended as, and should NOT be used as, a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Self-Assessment Tools

The PHQ-9 (Depression), GAD-7 (Anxiety) and PSS-10 (Stress) screening tools available on this website are validated screening instruments used as initial guides. They are:

  • NOT diagnostic tools — they cannot diagnose any condition
  • NOT a substitute for clinical evaluation by a qualified professional
  • General indicators only — scores should always be reviewed with a doctor
  • Not stored or transmitted — responses stay in your browser only

Always Consult a Professional

Never disregard or delay seeking professional medical advice because of something you read on this website. Always consult a qualified psychiatrist, psychologist, or physician for diagnosis and treatment of any medical condition.

No Doctor-Patient Relationship

Use of this website does not create a doctor-patient relationship between you and Affinity Hospital or any of its doctors. A formal doctor-patient relationship is established only through in-person or online consultation with one of our clinicians.

Accuracy of Information

While we strive to provide accurate and up-to-date information, medical knowledge evolves rapidly. We make no warranties about the completeness, accuracy, or suitability of information on this website. Always verify critical medical information with a qualified professional.
